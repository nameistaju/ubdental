Frequently Asked Questions

Visit a dentist if you have tooth pain, bleeding gums, sensitivity, missing teeth, bad breath, or if it has been more than six months since your last checkup.

Modern root canal treatment is designed to be comfortable. Local anesthesia and gentle techniques help relieve pain while saving the natural tooth.

Dental implants are a strong, natural-looking option for many patients with missing teeth. A dental scan helps determine bone support and treatment suitability.

Cosmetic dentistry may include teeth whitening, veneers, bonding, crowns, aligner planning, and complete smile makeovers based on your goals.
